Lasagna

Look at this mouth-watering Lasagna, this is what your Lasagna would be looking like in just an hour!

Description

Lasagna is both a type of wide, flat pasta and a popular baked dish made by layering that pasta with various fillings like meat sauce, cheese, and vegetables. It's a classic Italian dish that can be found in many cuisines worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Want to make it? Here is the recipe below!

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce with meat, veggies and tomato sauce.
  2. Let this simmer and boil the noodles and shred the cheeses.
  3. Just put them together!
  4. Bake until golden and crispy and cheesy :D
  5. Serve fresh!
