Description

Pasta is a dish originally from Italy consisting of dough made from durum wheat, extruded or stamped into various shapes and cooked in boiling water, and typically served with a sauce.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt

Want to make it? Here is the recipe below!

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce with eggs, veggies and flour.
  2. Let this simmer and boil the noodles and shred the cheeses.
  3. Just put them together!
  4. Cook until golden and smooth and cheesy :D
  5. Serve fresh!
